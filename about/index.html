About Southern Habitat Consulting

Southern Habitat Consulting is a South Alabama company that works directly on private land and active operating sites.

We help landowners manage timber, wildlife habitat, prescribed fire, invasive plants and long-term property improvements. We also provide vegetation-management services for industrial, municipal and utility properties.

Our work is not limited to inspections and written plans. We work in the field with tractors, skid steers, skidders, sprayers and other equipment suited to the job. We carry out vegetation treatments, property improvements and recurring maintenance. We also map the work and monitor what happens afterward.

Every project starts with the property itself. We look at current conditions and listen to what the client needs. Then we build the scope and get to work.

What the client can expect: A local company that plans the work, completes it in the field and stays involved through documentation and follow-up.

Mose Franklin

Mose Franklin believes the best way to understand a property is to spend time on it.

He works directly with private landowners and with industrial, municipal and utility clients. He helps define the job and stays involved as it moves into the field. That may mean walking treatment areas, operating equipment, checking completed work or reviewing what needs to happen next.

How We Approach Projects

We start by seeing the property and talking with the client.

The site review helps us understand access, vegetation and current conditions. It also helps us define the work before personnel and equipment are sent into the field.

Once the scope is clear, we move into implementation. That may involve mechanical treatment, targeted herbicide work, habitat improvement, site preparation, prescribed fire, monitoring or recurring maintenance. The method depends on the property and the job.

We document the work as it is completed. Records may include GIS files, GPS points, photographs and field notes. We also return for follow-up when the treatment or property calls for it.

What the client can expect: A direct process that moves from site review to field work, documentation and follow-up.
