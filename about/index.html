About Southern Habitat Consulting

Work That Serves the Property Today and Builds Value for the Future

Southern Habitat Consulting is a South Alabama company focused on practical land and vegetation management.

For private landowners, the work is often about more than the next project. It is about building a property that can be enjoyed, managed and passed down. That may mean developing a premier hunting property, improving wildlife habitat or managing timber as a long-term investment.

For industrial, municipal and utility clients, the focus is safe and efficient field work. We help plan and carry out vegetation-management projects around active sites, public property, drainage systems, rights-of-way and other working areas.

In every case, we start by understanding the property and the client's goals. We then define the work, complete it in the field and document what was done.

How We Approach Projects

We start by seeing the property and talking with the client.

The site review helps us understand access, vegetation and current conditions. It also helps us define the work before personnel and equipment are sent into the field.

Once the scope is clear, we move into implementation. That may involve mechanical treatment, targeted herbicide work, habitat improvement, site preparation, prescribed fire, monitoring or recurring maintenance. The method depends on the property and the job.

We document the work as it is completed. Records may include GIS files, GPS points, photographs and field notes. We also return for follow-up when the treatment or property calls for it.

What the client can expect: A direct process that moves from site review to field work, documentation and follow-up.
