About Southern Habitat Consulting

Science Based Wildlife Management—expanded into complete land and vegetation programs.

Wildlife biology is at the core of Southern Habitat Consulting. That science-based foundation guides how the company plans, coordinates, implements, and documents land, habitat, forestry, and vegetation work across South Alabama and Northwest Florida.

Built for the Southeast

Management grounded in the conditions of the Gulf Coast.

Southern Habitat Consulting works in pine forests, hardwood drains, wetlands, wildlife openings, rights-of-way, municipal properties, industrial acreage, and other landscapes where vegetation and natural resources require active management.

The company was built to close the gap between receiving recommendations and actually getting work completed. SHC can evaluate a property or site, establish priorities, define the scope, coordinate or perform fieldwork, track costs, and document results.

Our technical foundation includes wildlife biology, habitat management, forestry practices, vegetation control, prescribed fire, GIS mapping, monitoring, and natural-resource planning.

Who We Serve

Different clients, the same practical standard.

Every engagement is organized around the property, the desired outcome, the constraints on the ground, and work that can be clearly measured.

Private Landowners

Long-term programs connecting timber, wildlife, habitat, prescribed fire, invasive plants, roads, surveys, and annual budgets.

Industrial & Utility Clients

Vegetation control, access maintenance, invasive-species work, mowing, clearing, monitoring, and documented maintenance cycles.

Municipal & Public Agencies

Natural-area management, public-property vegetation work, field assessment, mapping, recurring services, and contract support.

How We Work

One management process from problem to completion.

01

02

03
