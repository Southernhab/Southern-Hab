About Southern Habitat

Advice is only valuable when it leads to practical work.

Southern Habitat Consulting connects natural-resource judgment with the planning, field execution, and documentation needed to improve land and maintain operational sites.

Local knowledge. Professional judgment.

Built for the working landscapes of the Gulf Coast.

SHC is based in Bay Minette, Alabama and serves private, municipal, industrial, and utility clients across South Alabama and Northwest Florida.

Founder Richard “Mose” Franklin brings a wildlife ecology and land-management background together with municipal arboriculture, vegetation assessment, GIS, field operations, and public-sector project experience.

  • B.S. Wildlife Ecology & Management, Auburn University
  • ISA Certified Arborist
  • Tree Risk Assessment Qualified
  • Municipal tree and landscape review experience
  • Wildlife, forestry, habitat, and vegetation fieldwork

How Southern Habitat is different.

We connect the pieces.

Timber, wildlife, vegetation, fire, roads, budgets, and timing are treated as one property or site—not unrelated jobs.

We stay practical.

Recommendations are prioritized by impact, feasibility, cost, operational need, and the conditions actually present.

We document the work.

Clients receive clear scopes, field records, photographs, maps, findings, and next-step recommendations.
