Reforestation and Longleaf-Pine Establishment

Reforestation requires planning before planting and follow-up after seedlings are in the ground.

We begin by reviewing the site and the owner's long-term goals. Existing vegetation, access and previous land use all affect the project. Longleaf pine may require a different preparation and maintenance approach than other species.

We move the project from site preparation into planting and early stand care. Work may include treatment layout, planting coordination, survival checks and competing-vegetation control. We can also track the planting area with GIS mapping and field records. Qualified forestry support is included when the project requires registered-forester services.

What the Client Can Expect

Active project management from site preparation through planting, survival checks and early vegetation control.
