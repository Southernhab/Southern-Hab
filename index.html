Practical Land and Vegetation Management

Southern Habitat Consulting provides vegetation-management services for industrial, municipal and utility properties. We also work with private landowners on timber management, wildlife habitat, prescribed fire, invasive plants and long-term property improvements.

Most projects begin with a site review. We assess current conditions and discuss the client's priorities before developing recommendations or defining the scope of work.

Our approach is practical and site-specific. Every recommendation is based on the property, the client's objectives and the conditions on the ground.
