Practical Solutions for Private and Public Land Management

Southern Habitat Consulting helps private landowners manage timber, wildlife habitat, prescribed fire, invasive plants and long-term property improvements.

We also provide vegetation-management services for industrial, municipal and utility properties. Our field work may include mowing, mechanical clearing, targeted herbicide treatment, invasive-plant control and recurring maintenance.

We do more than inspect a property and provide recommendations. We develop the scope, complete the work in the field and document what was done.

Every project is built around the property, the client's goals and the conditions on the ground.
