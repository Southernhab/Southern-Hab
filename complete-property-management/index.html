Service Description

Some landowners need more than help with one project. They need someone to keep the full property plan moving.

We begin with the owner's long-term goals and the current condition of the land. We then organize the work by season and priority. That may include forestry work, habitat improvements, invasive-plant control, wildlife monitoring and prescribed fire.

Southern Habitat Consulting stays involved as the work is carried out. We schedule field visits, complete treatments, update maps and track results. The owner has one point of contact and one management direction for the property.

What the Client Can Expect

Ongoing field work, monitoring, mapping and annual priorities organized under one practical property-management plan.
