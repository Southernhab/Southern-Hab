Private Land Services

Private land usually has more than one management need. Timber, wildlife, habitat, roads, prescribed fire and invasive plants often affect one another.

We begin by walking the property and talking through what the owner wants to accomplish. We then identify the most important needs and place the work in a practical order.

Southern Habitat Consulting does more than prepare plans. We carry out field work, manage treatment areas, monitor results and keep projects moving from one stage to the next.
