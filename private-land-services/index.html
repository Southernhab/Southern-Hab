Private Lands

Coordinated Management for the Entire Property

Effective land management requires more than addressing each issue separately. Timber, habitat, wildlife, access, fire and invasive plants often affect one another.

We begin by evaluating the property and discussing the landowner's goals. We then identify the most important needs and organize the work in a practical order.

The result is a plan based on the property and the owner's priorities. It is not a standard program applied to every tract.

Property Assessment and Planning

A property assessment provides a clear starting point. We evaluate timber, vegetation, wildlife habitat, roads, drainage and previous management work, then identify priorities and outline next steps.
