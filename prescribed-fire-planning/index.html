Prescribed-Fire Planning and Management

← Back to Private-Land Services

Service Description

Prescribed fire can improve habitat, manage unwanted vegetation and support long-term timber goals.

We review potential burn units, existing firebreaks and access routes. We also consider roads, structures, nearby property and smoke-sensitive areas. The owner's goals help determine where fire should be used and how often it should return.

Our work may include burn-unit layout, firebreak preparation, timing, field implementation and post-burn review. We document completed work and monitor how the vegetation responds. Future burn plans can then be adjusted as the property and management goals change.

What the Client Can Expect

Burn units reviewed and prepared in the field, followed by organized implementation, documentation and future management planning.
