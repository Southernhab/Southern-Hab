← Back to Private-Land Services

Service Description

Invasive plants can reduce timber growth, crowd out desirable vegetation and make parts of the property harder to use.

We identify the target species and determine how far it has spread. We also look at nearby water, desirable plants and property access. The treatment method is selected based on the plant and the conditions on the ground.

Field work may include mowing, cutting, mechanical treatment or herbicide application. Established infestations usually require monitoring and repeated treatment. We can map affected areas and maintain treatment records so the owner can see where work has been completed and what still needs attention.

What the Client Can Expect

Problem areas identified, mapped and treated in the field, with follow-up work scheduled when continued control is needed.
