Complete Property Management

Some landowners need more than help with one project. They need someone to keep the entire property moving toward a long-term goal.

We build a management plan around the owner’s vision for the land. That may include timber work, habitat improvements, invasive-plant control, prescribed fire, wildlife monitoring, access improvements and seasonal maintenance. We organize the work by priority, timing and budget so each project supports the next.

Southern Habitat Consulting stays involved from planning through field implementation. We schedule site visits, complete treatments, update maps and track results over time. We also help eligible landowners work through NRCS cost-share programs such as EQIP. These programs may help offset the cost of approved management practices and allow owners to complete more work across the property.

The result is one clear direction for the land and one point of contact who understands the full plan. Instead of treating every issue as a separate job, we manage the property as a whole and keep progress moving year after year.

What the Client Can Expect

A long-term property-management plan that brings together field work, annual priorities, GIS mapping, monitoring, project scheduling and assistance with eligible NRCS cost-share opportunities.
