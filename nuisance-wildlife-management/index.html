Service Description

Wildlife damage often continues when the conditions attracting the animal are not addressed.

We inspect the affected area and look for damage, tracks, entry points and repeated activity. We also review nearby food and shelter. This helps us identify what is causing the problem and what field work may be needed.

Services may include monitoring, exclusion measures, property changes or targeted removal where authorized. Follow-up visits can be used to confirm whether the activity has stopped or moved to another area. The work is based on the species, the type of damage and the conditions present on the property.

What the Client Can Expect

An on-site assessment followed by practical field work aimed at solving the problem and reducing repeated activity.
