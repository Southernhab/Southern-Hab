Property Assessment and Planning

A landowner may know that a property needs work without knowing where to begin. A field assessment helps separate immediate needs from projects that can be handled over time.

We walk the property and review timber, habitat, roads, trails, drainage, firebreaks and invasive plants. We also look at previous management work and current land use. The owner's goals guide the review.

After the assessment, we organize the next steps and begin moving the work forward. Some items may call for direct field treatment. Others may involve mapping, monitoring or seasonal scheduling. We can divide the work into short-term and long-term priorities so the owner has a clear path ahead.

What the Client Can Expect

A field-based review, mapped areas of interest and a practical work plan that can be put into action.
